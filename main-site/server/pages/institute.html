Afternoon Classes

Students

90 students, with free tuition and transportation, will have the opportunity to learn the important subjects such as Math, English, Arabic, Science, Social-emotional Support, and Bible so they are developed holistically as a person

Stable Work and Pay

Teachers

When Lebanon is not paying her teachers, we step in

80% of our budget goes to our teachers' salary. At $250 a month, you can provide a livable wage to a teacher who can bring home enough pay to support her family.

Facility

We have secured classrooms and staff

Our ministry partner, the Syriac Orthodox Diocese of Baalbeck and Zahle, has provided us with access to their facility at practically no cost. As foreign missionaries leave Lebanon with their donor base, we step in to hire local teachers so the work of education can continue.
