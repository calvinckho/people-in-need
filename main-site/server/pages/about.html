How It Started

Calvin visited Lebanon in January 2020 and stayed in Fady's house. They have developed a strong friendship since and decided to start this project together to help Christians in need in the Middle East.

Calvin began an appeal to give in March 2020. He was able to raise $3500 in a month. He sent the money to Fady in 3 installments, and as each batch of donation arrives, Fady would go and procure food and other supplies.

Fady has since been building a strong partnership with a local Syriac Orthodox Church in Zahle. They have allowed Fady to use the church as the distribution center so Christian families can come and pick up the supplies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help, serve, and bless as the hands of Jesus. We truly believe that our work can draw many's hearts toward God as He says He will not forget His people.
